About Us

Couloir Classes is- a world-class guide team organize and lead climbs, treks and mountaineering expeditions around the world, from the classic climbs of the Cascades to the 5,000m peaks of the Alps. We are proud to be one of the most respected and active guiding companies in the world. Since 1986 we have conducted thousands of expeditions to destinations around the globe, and the fact that most of our customers climb with us again and again tells us we're doing things right.
